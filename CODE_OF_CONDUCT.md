# Code of Conduct

Open Data Fusion contributors are expected to create a professional, inclusive, and technically rigorous community.

Be respectful, assume good intent, give actionable review feedback, protect confidential operational data, and keep disagreement focused on evidence and design trade-offs. Harassment, discriminatory conduct, threats, deliberate disclosure of private information, and unsafe handling of industrial credentials or plant data are not acceptable.

Project maintainers may remove content or participation that violates these expectations. Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers.
